ABOUT THE CAFE

Django Cafe was founded by Mr. Jethro Jabay. Our dedication to the perfect cup of coffee is unwavering. We source premium coffee beans from around the world, ensuring each sip is a journey of flavors. From robust espresso shots to creamy lattes and exotic pour-over options, our menu is a curated selection designed to cater to both connoisseurs and those new to the world of specialty coffee.

Beyond being a coffee destination, Django Cafe is a hub of activity. Join us for coffee tastings, live music nights, and community events that bring people together.
